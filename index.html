WordSENSEI

Repo | Report

WordSENSEI is a set of algorithms for word sense disambiguation (WSD), an ongoing problem in natural language processing (NLP). WSD is the task of determining the sense of an ambiguous word (a word with multiple senses, like "bank" or "mouse") within a sentence. Our system is unique in that it uses Wikipedia as its corpus (dictionary). You can read more at the report linked above.

XPify

Repo

(In need of a better name)

XPify is a web application to "gamify" classroom learning. Gamification is "the application of typical elements of game playing (e.g., point scoring, competition with others, rules of play) to other areas of activity" (Google), and XPify will use a point-based system to reward students for completing "challenges" defined by the teacher.
